## Privacy Policy for Drone Wars

**Last updated: January 2026**

Thank you for playing Drone Wars. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect (none) and how the game works.

### Data Collection

Drone Wars does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress, high scores, and settings are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

### Third-Party Services

Drone Wars integrates with **Apple Game Center** for leaderboards on iOS. Leaderboard scores submitted through Game Center are governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We have no access to your Game Center data.

Other than Game Center, this game uses no third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or tracking SDKs.

### Account & Sign-In

No account or sign-in is required to play Drone Wars. The game works entirely offline.

### Data Deletion

Uninstalling the game from your device permanently deletes all locally stored data. Game Center scores are managed through your Apple ID — you can delete them via the Game Center app.

### Children's Privacy

Drone Wars does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

### Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date.

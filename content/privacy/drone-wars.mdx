# Privacy Policy — Drone Wars

**Last updated: April 2, 2026**

---

## Overview

Drone Wars is a fast-paced mobile space shooter. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

**Short version: We don't require any personal information to play. Some non-personal usage data may be collected to improve the experience.**

---

## What We Collect

### Data stored on your device only
All of the following is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. It never leaves your phone and is never sent to our servers:

- Your high scores and game progress
- Your game settings and preferences

### Non-personal usage data
We may collect non-personal technical information to improve the game experience:

- Device model and operating system version
- General game performance data

### Data we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
- We do not require you to create an account
- We do not track your location

---

## Third-Party Services

Our app includes the following third-party services that may collect data:

### Google Play Services
Used for achievements and leaderboards on Android. Governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### AdMob
Used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect your Advertising ID to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. You can reset or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings. Governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Expo / React Native
Standard technical infrastructure required for the app to function. Minimal technical logs are generated as part of normal app operation.

---

## Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information and use commercially acceptable means to do so. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Children's Privacy

Drone Wars is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will immediately delete it from our servers.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

# Privacy Policy — Quite Wins

**Last updated: April 1, 2026**

---

## Overview

Quite Wins is a personal win tracking app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

**Short version: We collect almost nothing. Everything stays on your device.**

---

## What We Collect

### Data stored on your device only

All of the following is stored **locally on your device** and never leaves it:

- Your daily wins (text and emoji you enter)
- Your streak and win count
- Your garden progress (plants, seasons, elders)
- Your notification preferences
- Your theme and sound preferences
- Your in-app purchase status (unlocked scenes and palettes)

### Data we do not collect

- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information
- We do not create user accounts
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
- We do not share data with third parties, except as described in the In-App Purchases section below

---

## In-App Purchases

Quite Wins offers optional one-time in-app purchases (Night & Space Bundle, Grove Palettes Bundle, Everything Bundle).

Purchases are processed by:

- **Google Play** (Android) — governed by Google's Privacy Policy
- **Apple App Store** (iOS) — governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

We use **RevenueCat** to manage purchase verification. RevenueCat may process your purchase receipt to verify entitlements. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

We do not store your payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google or Apple.

---

## Notifications

If you enable notifications, your chosen notification time is stored locally on your device only. Notifications are scheduled on-device — they do not require a server connection and no notification content is transmitted to or from our servers.

---

## Data Deletion

Since all personal data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. This permanently removes all your wins, garden progress, and settings from your device.

---

## Children's Privacy

Quite Wins is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used this app, please contact us at the address below.
